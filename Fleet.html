Fleet

  • Boeing
    The Boeing Company, founded in 1916 at a small airfield in the Seattle, Washington state by William E Boeing and started designing and building commercial and military aircraft all the way up to this day with high tech 787 Dreamliners and 737 Max
  • De Haviland
    De Haviland was a British aircraft manufacturer founded in 1920 at Edgware aerodrome on the outskirts of London by Geoffrey de Havilland. They later moved their operations to Hatfield.
  • Bristol Company
    the Bristol Aeroplane Company was founded in 1910 at Filton airport in Bristol. Filton became the home of BOAC during the war years in the time between Croydon airport closing to Heathrow opening
